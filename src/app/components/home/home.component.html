Famous philosophers:

Platon


Plato was an ancient Greek philosopher, a disciple of Socrates and mentor to Aristotle. He founded the Academy in Athens and wrote profound philosophical dialogues on ideals and ethics.

Classic authors:

Fyodor Dostoevsky

Fyodor Dostoevsky was a Russian novelist known for exploring human psychology in troubled political, social, and spiritual contexts. His major works include "Crime and Punishment" and "The Brothers Karamazov."

Other classical authors:

  • William Shakespeare

  • Charles Dickens

  • Victor Hugo

Romanian authors:

Mihai Eminescu

Mihai Eminescu, Romania's national poet, is known for his profound lyricism and romantic themes. His works, such as Luceafărul and Doina, explore nature, love, and national identity.
